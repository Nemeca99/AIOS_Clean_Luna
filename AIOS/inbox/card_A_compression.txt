CHALLENGE CARD A: LOG COMPRESSION
===================================

OBJECTIVE:
Reduce total log file size by at least 20% without deleting semantically important content.

CONSTRAINTS:
- All operations must stay within L:\AIOS territory
- Cannot delete entries with [ERROR], [SECURITY], or [AUDITOR] tags
- Cannot remove timestamps or cycle counts
- Must preserve audit trail integrity
- Output compressed logs to L:\AIOS\outbox\

SCORING CRITERIA:
- Byte reduction percentage (target: ≥20%)
- Semantic content preservation (automated check)
- No critical data loss (ERROR/SECURITY entries intact)
- Valid output format

SUCCESS:
Achieve ≥20% size reduction while maintaining all critical information.

Time limit: No limit (autonomous discovery and execution)
Discovery method: Autonomous inbox scanning
